    BORN FROM OUR LOVE OF GOOD COFFEE, COLD BEER AND FINE FOOD. IT'S BEEN QUITE A JOURNEY.

June 18, 2026

A note from The Smoking Lion

We wanted to let you know that The Smoking Lion is closed for the time being, for personal reasons.

Creating the D’ash Bowl and getting it into your hands has been one of the most rewarding things we’ve ever done. Every order, every kind word, every photo you sent us — it meant the world, and it still does.

For now, we’re stepping back to focus on what matters most. The website will stay right here as a home for the story and everything we built together.

Thank you, truly, for being part of this. It was never just an ashtray — it was a community, and you made it one.

With gratitude,
The Smoking Lion

Introducing the Ultimate Stoner Ash Tray – The D’ash Bowl!

Experience a revolutionary way to smoke herb with the D’ash Bowl, specifically designed to meet and exceed all your expectations. Crafted from heat-resistant silicone, this solid and non-flammable ashtray is here to elevate your smoking sessions. As seen in Leafly’s Best of Cannabis Products, the D’ash Bowl offers the ultimate convenience of ashing without even leaving your couch! However, there’s much more to this little ashtray than meets the eye. Having extensively tested and researched the D’ash Bowl, here are the top 5 reasons why it stands as the best ashtray for cannabis enthusiasts.

1. Effortless Cleaning

No need for tedious cleaning sessions. The D’ash Bowl can be effortlessly cleaned by simply tossing it in the dishwasher. It is made from FDA-approved, food-grade silicone, ensuring its dishwasher-friendly nature. Warm water and soap work equally well for quick cleaning.

2. Unbreakable Durability

Say goodbye to the fear of breakage or punctures. The D’ash Bowl exhibits incredible flexibility and resilience. It can withstand accidental drops without a scratch, even surviving a three-story fall. The debowler spike in the center ensures safety while maintaining the ashtray’s durable and flexible material.

3. Discreet and Portable

The compact size of the D’ash Bowl allows for discreet storage in small drawers found in most bathrooms and kitchens. Additionally, its round dimensions make it a portable gift that can easily be carried anywhere.

4. Aesthetically Pleasing Design

The D’ash Bowl boasts a modern and symmetrical design that stands out as an aesthetically pleasing piece. Many customers enjoy showcasing it on their coffee or end tables, treating it like a work of art. Furthermore, its ability to rest solidly on surfaces makes it perfect for lounging on your bed with friends.

5. Versatile Functionality

This ashtray caters to all types of cannabis enthusiasts. Whether you’re a joint smoker, a bong user, a dabber, or a hookah lover, the D’ash Bowl serves all your needs. The contoured grooves provide a resting spot for joints, while pipe stems can be cleared of resin and ash with ease. It even accommodates dab rig tools and can safely hold burning coal for hookah sessions. See a short demo video showing this here.
